Pizza

Description

A delicious pizza baked in an specialized pizza oven made with fresh and top quality ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Dough (secret recipe)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Sausage
  • Pepperoni
  • Black olives
  • Red Onion

Cooking steps

  1. Roll dough
  2. Spread sauce
  3. Spread cheese
  4. Add toppings
  5. Bake at 450F for 10 minutes
  6. Cut pizza and let it cool for 5 minutes
